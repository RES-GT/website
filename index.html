RES@GT Bootcamp is a 12-week design-thinking intensive for Georgia Tech students that serves as a stepping stone into the world of entrepreneurship. Following an innovative, action-oriented process, students will learn to identify and prototype impactful solutions to problems they’re interested in tackling. Bootcamp culminates in a final pitch presentation, in which teams of students pitch their startup ideas to a panel of investors. This is your chance to join Georgia Tech’s closest-knit startup community!
